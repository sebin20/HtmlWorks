The early morning mist clung to the trees as the sun began to rise, casting a warm, golden glow across the landscape. Birds chirped in the distance, their songs a peaceful soundtrack to the stillness of the day’s beginning. The air was cool but carried the promise of warmth, a gentle reminder that the world was waking up, full of possibility. As the light grew stronger, the mist slowly dissipated, revealing the vibrant colors of autumn—the reds, yellows, and oranges of the trees standing out against the soft blue sky. It was a moment of quiet beauty, a perfect pause before the rush of the day began.
